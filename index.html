My name is Luca Monfre, I am an artist from Milwaukee, WI working in multiple mediums that cover a vast array of disciplines. Most of my arts practice focuses on digital life and its relationship to organic life, repression, desire, and neuroticism. my goal is to define exactly what about commercial technologies create fascistic seeds within the brain, and how to free oneself from the social mechanisms of the internet.
