My name is Luca Monfre, I am an artist from Milwaukee, WI and I enjoy working in multiple mediums that cover a vast selection of disciplines. Most of my artistic practice focuses around the flaws and imperfections of "mathemetically perfect" digital media formats, their innerworkings, algorithms, and the human relationship to said concepts. I especially have an interest in compression and the self immolating nature of lossy media.
