Luca Monfre is a year old multidisciplinary artist from Milwaukee, WI. Luca Monfre studied at the Milwaukee Institute of Art and Design. Luca Monfre is an artist who focuses on the appropriation of digital imagery and aesthetics to represent the interpersonal relationships they have with others, themself and the media they grew up with. LucaÅfs art heavily centers around their interests, hobbies, and comforts as well as how they can twist them into something new or representational, often borrowing imagery from their inspirations directly. Luca has previously had their works shown in "The Human_body tomorrow" exhibition at Some Fools Basement, the digital nostalgia exhibition at Between Two Galleries as well as in the MIAD Senior Thesis exhibition.
