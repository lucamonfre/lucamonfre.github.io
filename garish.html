'garish despite efforts' is an expression of frustration with presentation, specifically, with finding a mismatch in one's outward appearance and ones desire for it to be different. frustration with a plea for an ideal image of professionalism and serious dialogue, and the reality of being a crude, cartoony character dressed in ugly clashing colors reflective of ones immature, childish nature at heart.
